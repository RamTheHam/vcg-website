About

Built from 20 years inside PE

Founded by Henrik Rammer after two decades in PE across the Nordics and Europe. The observation: the best operators hold deal intelligence PE firms never access through traditional channels.

Executive search finds people for deals. Investment banks find deals for buyers. Neither starts where the real signal lives.

VCG fills the gap. Not a search firm. Not an advisory. A deal origination partner that starts with the person who holds the intelligence.

Based in Stockholm. Independent, founder-led, built on twenty years of direct operator relationships.

The method

Operator-first, by design

Every opportunity begins with a structured conversation — not a CV, not a database search. We draw out the operator's deal thesis, their read on the sector, and the specific company they know is mispriced or mismanaged.

Behavioural profiling captures how each operator thinks, decides, and leads — intelligence that would take a PE firm months to surface post-close. We package it to investment-committee standard before any PE firm sees the deal.
