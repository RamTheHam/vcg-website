The diagnosis

It's hard for external CEOs to get in the driving seat.

The standard process is built around the PE firm and the auction: identify the target, run the bid, close the transaction, keep the incumbent management. Replacing the CEO at close is risky and rare.

For an executive who wants to drive a PE-backed platform, that path is closed. The route opens from the other side — walking in with the company, the thesis, and the value creation plan already shaped.

Drive the process from the executive from the beginning.

VCG originates from that side. The executive brings sector intelligence and operating judgment. VCG brings PE access, methodology, and capital. Together we shape the deal and route it where it fits. The executive enters as the deal's author, in the role it requires.

The process

Three stages. Focused.

01 — Draw out

A structured conversation with the executive.

20-odd years of operating experience, sector view, ambition, network, what they would do differently if they owned it. Then find the deal that can carry it.

02 — Shape and take to market

VCG shapes the thesis into a PE-grade package.

We identify the PE firms whose mandate and culture fit. We negotiate the full structure — role, authority, compensation, equity, governance — so the executive can stay focused on the opportunity itself.

03 — Invest alongside

At close, we each reinvest half our origination fee back into the company.

That is the alignment signal — to the PE firm and to each other — that we believe in this enough to put our own capital on the same terms.

How a thesis is built

Three stages. From your sector view to a deal package a PE firm can underwrite.

Stage 1

Sector intelligence

  • Your 20-year sector view
  • The inefficiency only you've seen
  • Your network of insiders and customers
  • The hypothesis: "X kind of company can become Y"

Stage 2

Thesis frame

  • Target type, size, and geography
  • Value creation plan, sequenced
  • Your role at close — CEO, chair, operating partner
  • Equity and governance terms

Stage 3

Deal package

  • Specific target identified or pipelined
  • PE firms mapped to mandate fit
  • Role and equity terms drafted
  • VCG co-invest committed at close
